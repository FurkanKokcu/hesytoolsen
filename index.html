About

Hesy Tools is a digital assistant tool designed to gather information and calculations frequently needed in dental practice into a single interface. It mathematically performs hematological risk analysis and periodontal staging, while serving as a quick-access digital reference for frequently used prescription protocols and anamnesis terms. I developed it to speed up complex processes and allow access to practical information in seconds.
