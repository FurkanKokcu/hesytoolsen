About

Hesy Tools is a digital assistant tool that gathers frequently needed information and calculations in dentistry practice into a single interface. While mathematically calculating hematological risk analysis and periodontal staging, it serves as a quick-access digital reference source for frequently used prescription protocols and anamnesis terms. I developed it to speed up complex processes and access practical information in seconds.
